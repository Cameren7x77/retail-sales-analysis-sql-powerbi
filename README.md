# 📈 Business Questions

The project answers the following business questions:

1. How many orders are contained within the dataset?
2. How many products are available?
3. What is the total revenue generated?
4. Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
5. Which products generate the highest revenue?
6. Which supplier countries generate the highest revenue?
7. How has revenue changed over time?

---

# 📊 Key Findings

- **185,013** customer orders analysed.
- **5,504** products analysed.
- Total revenue exceeded **£25.64 Million**.
- **Outdoors** was the highest revenue-generating product category.
- **Family Holiday 4** generated the highest individual product revenue.
